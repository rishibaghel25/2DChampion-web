Welcome to 2D Champion!

Play, Compete, and Win!

2D Champion is a platform where gamers can come together to play and compete in 2D games. Our website features a leaderboard where users can see their high scores and challenge others. The game repository is open source, allowing users to contribute and enhance the gaming experience. The top 3 players on the leaderboard win amazing prizes.
